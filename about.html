About Us

Dr. Rahul Sharma MBBS, DNB (Medicine), DNB (Cardiology)

Speciality: Cardiology, Interventional Cardiology

Qualifications: MBBS, DNB (Medicine), DNB (Cardiology)

Fellowships:

  • Society for Cardiovascular Angiography and Interventions (SCAI). New Delhi.
  • European Bifurcation Club. Mumbai.
Dr. Rahul Sharma is an Interventional Cardiologist in Jaipur practising at Fortis Escorts Hospital, Jaipur. He treats a variety of cardiovascular disorders such as myocardial infarction (heart attack), angina pectoris, peripheral vascular diseases, arrhythmias, LV dysfunction, heart failure, etc. Dr. Sharma also visits several other hospitals and clinics in Jaipur and nearby towns on specified days.
Dr. Rahul Sharma follows a holistic approach to treating his patients and believes that prevention is better than cure. He is proficient in coronary angioplasty, peripheral angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, and workup for cardiac transplant cases.
